Overview

Tax Credits & Incentives

Governments reward the work you're already doing — building software, refining processes, retaining staff through disruption, and hiring from targeted groups — but the credits go unclaimed when the paperwork feels daunting. AccountsUp identifies every incentive your business qualifies for across the US and Canada, quantifies the benefit precisely, and files claims backed by the contemporaneous evidence tax authorities expect to see.

The difference between a credit that sticks and one that's clawed back is documentation. Our teams pair experienced credit specialists with AI-assisted review to build the technical narratives, expenditure schedules, and wage substantiation that hold up under IRS or CRA scrutiny. You claim confidently now, and you can defend easily later if a reviewer ever comes knocking with an information request.

Whether you're a CPA firm that needs specialist capacity for client R&D studies or a growing company claiming SR&ED for the first time, we work inside your existing stack — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, and ADP — pulling the payroll and ledger data credits depend on without disrupting your close or forcing a migration.

Amended Returns & Look-Back Claims

Retroactive credit recovery for open tax years — amended returns and carryforward tracking that reclaim benefits from R&D activity you never claimed.

Questions

Tax Credits & Incentives, answered

Which tax credits does AccountsUp help capture?

We cover US R&D credits under IRC §41 (Form 6765), the Work Opportunity Tax Credit, Employee Retention Credit review and defense, and Canadian SR&ED (Form T661), plus related state and provincial incentives — identifying every program your activity qualifies for across the US and Canada.

How do you make sure a claim will hold up under an IRS or CRA audit?

We build contemporaneous documentation — technical narratives, time allocations, expenditure schedules, and wage substantiation — while the work is fresh, following the exact four-part test and CRA criteria reviewers apply. Every file is assembled as an indexed, audit-ready binder, and we support you through any examination.

Can you still help if we already claimed ERTC or missed prior R&D credits?

Yes. We re-substantiate existing ERTC claims and prepare Form 941-X or audit-defense packages, and we file amended returns to recover R&D credits from open tax years you never claimed. Carryforwards from prior activity are tracked and applied too.

Will this disrupt our current accounting workflow or software?

No. We work inside the systems you already use — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, and ADP — pulling payroll and ledger data directly. There is no migration and no interruption to your monthly close; your team keeps working exactly as before.

How is our financial and payroll data protected?

All data is handled under enterprise-grade controls: encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access limited to your engagement team, multi-factor authentication, and complete audit trails. Work happens in secure, office-based environments aligned to SOC 2 and GDPR standards.

How long does a credit engagement take to get started?

Onboarding typically takes a few days once system access is granted. From there, timelines depend on scope — a single-year R&D or SR&ED study moves faster than a multi-year look-back — and we give you a milestone schedule up front so there are no surprises.
