Overview

Order-to-Cash (O2C)

Order-to-cash is where revenue you've already earned either becomes cash in the bank or quietly ages into a write-off. AccountsUp gives you a dedicated team that owns the full cycle — clean invoicing, structured collections, precise cash application, and tight reconciliation — so the gap between delivering work and getting paid stops widening and starts shrinking, month after month.

We work inside the tools you already run — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, and Bill.com — so there's no rip-and-replace and no new system to learn. AI-assisted matching clears cash and flags exceptions in minutes, while experienced analysts handle the judgment calls: disputed invoices, short payments, and the tough collections conversations that automation alone can't close.

Whether you're a CPA firm carrying client receivables or a growing business scaling past spreadsheets, you get board-ready aging reports, a documented collections cadence, and a DSO number that finally trends down. Everything runs from secure, office-based operations with full audit trails, so faster cash never comes at the cost of control or compliance.

  • A steadily falling DSO you can defend in front of your board
  • Cleaner, faster month-end closes with receivables that tie out to the ledger
  • Fewer write-offs as disputes and deductions get worked to root cause
  • Predictable cash flow from disciplined, on-cadence collections

What's included

Everything the order-to-cash (o2c) engagement covers

Invoicing & Billing

We generate, validate, and dispatch accurate invoices on your terms and schedule, catching pricing, tax, and PO errors before they reach the customer and delay payment.

Collections & Dunning

A structured, multi-touch collections cadence — reminders, statements, and escalations — worked by real analysts who prioritize the highest-value overdue accounts and keep every customer relationship intact.

Cash Application

AI-assisted matching applies incoming payments to open invoices — including partials, lockbox files, and remittance-free wires — so your ledger reflects true cash position the same day.

AR Reconciliation

We reconcile receivables against the general ledger and bank deposits every period, resolving variances and unapplied cash so month-end closes clean and your aging report ties out.

Deductions & Disputes

We investigate short pays, chargebacks, and deductions to root cause, documenting each case and recovering valid amounts instead of letting unexplained write-offs quietly erode your margin.

Credit & Customer Setup

We run credit checks, set risk-based limits, and onboard new customers with clean master data, so you extend terms deliberately rather than discovering exposure after the fact.

AR Reporting & Dashboards

Weekly aging, DSO trends, collector performance, and cash forecasts delivered in dashboards you can take straight into leadership meetings — no more assembling receivables data by hand.

DSO Reduction Program

A targeted program that diagnoses where days sales outstanding leaks — billing lag, dispute backlog, weak follow-up — then fixes the root causes, not just the symptoms, so the reduction holds.

Questions

Order-to-Cash (O2C), answered

How quickly can AccountsUp take over our order-to-cash process?

Most clients are fully transitioned within two to four weeks. We start with a discovery and AR audit, connect securely to your accounting stack, then run in parallel before taking full ownership — so billing and collections never pause during the handover.

Will you work in our existing accounting system, or make us switch?

We work inside the tools you already use — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, Bill.com, and connected ERPs. There's no migration and no new software to buy. You keep ownership of your systems and data; we operate within them using role-based access.

How do you actually reduce our DSO?

DSO usually leaks from three places: slow or inaccurate billing, unresolved disputes, and inconsistent follow-up. We fix all three — invoicing same-day and error-free, working deductions to root cause, and running a disciplined collections cadence — then report the trend so you can see days outstanding fall.

How is our financial and customer data kept secure?

Your data stays protected by SOC 2-aligned controls, role-based least-privilege access, multi-factor authentication, and encrypted connections. Our teams operate from secure, office-based facilities — never home networks — and every action is logged in an audit trail you can review at any time.

Will your team contact our customers directly for collections?

Only in the way you approve. We follow your branding, tone, and escalation rules, and can send communications from your domain or stay behind the scenes. Every reminder and call is documented, and difficult accounts are escalated to you before any relationship-sensitive step.

What does order-to-cash outsourcing cost?

Pricing scales with invoice and transaction volume, the sub-services you need, and the number of entities or currencies involved. Most clients choose a fixed monthly engagement rather than hourly billing, so costs are predictable. We'll scope your volumes in a consultation and share transparent, itemized pricing.
