Overview

Audit & Assurance Support

Audit season rarely fails because of the opinion — it fails in the preparation. Vouching support, tying trial balances to lead schedules, chasing signed confirmations, and rebuilding reconciliations that don't foot: these eat the hours that should go to judgment and review. AccountsUp takes that preparation off your plate. Our dedicated teams assemble audit-ready workpapers, tie every schedule back to the general ledger, and deliver a PBC list that arrives answered, indexed, and referenced — so your engagement team walks into fieldwork ready to test, not ready to reconcile.

We work as an extension of your audit or finance team, inside the files and platforms you already use — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, and your document-management or workpaper software. Every deliverable follows a consistent referencing convention, carries a clear audit trail, and is reviewed before it reaches your partner or your external auditors. AI-assisted checks flag foot errors, unreconciled variances, and missing support early; experienced accountants apply the judgment, tie-outs, and professional formatting that a machine cannot.

Whether you are a CPA firm smoothing a lumpy busy season or a growing company preparing for its first financial-statement audit, the goal is the same: fewer review notes, faster turnaround on PBC requests, and a workpaper package a reviewer can navigate without a single follow-up email.

  • Fewer reviewer and partner notes on preparation quality
  • Fieldwork that closes on the planned schedule instead of running into overtime
  • A PBC list that arrives answered and indexed rather than open
  • Busy-season capacity added without hiring or carrying off-season headcount

What's included

Everything the audit & assurance support engagement covers

Audit-Ready Workpapers

Fully indexed, cross-referenced workpaper sets — lead schedules, supporting detail, and tie-outs to the trial balance — formatted to your firm's templates and referencing standards.

Account Reconciliations & Tie-Outs

Bank, credit-card, intercompany, and balance-sheet reconciliations that foot and agree, with every reconciling item explained and supported so reviewers stop at 'signed off,' not 'explain this.'

Lead & Supporting Schedules

Confirmations & Vouching Support

Preparation and tracking of bank, AR, and AP confirmations and legal letters, plus pulling and organizing invoices, contracts, and support for detail testing and sampling.

Reviewer & Partner Support

Roll-Forward & Prior-Year Alignment

Prior-year workpapers rolled forward, opening balances agreed, and current-year variances flagged with explanations — so year-over-year movement is documented before it's questioned.

Consolidations & Adjusting Entries

Consolidation schedules, elimination entries, and audit-adjusting journal entries prepared and traced through to a supportable, reviewer-friendly final trial balance.

Questions

Audit & Assurance Support, answered

Do you replace our auditors or perform the audit itself?

No. We provide preparation and support — assembling audit-ready workpapers, reconciliations, schedules, and PBC items. Independence rules mean the audit opinion stays with your engagement team or external auditors; we make their fieldwork faster by handing them clean, tied-out, reviewer-ready files.

How do you keep our financial data secure?

We operate under enterprise-grade controls: role-based access so staff see only their assigned files, multi-factor authentication, encrypted data in transit and at rest, full audit trails on every action, and secured, office-based operations. We work inside your systems, so sensitive data stays in your environment rather than being copied out.

Which accounting and workpaper platforms do you work in?

We work directly inside QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, and ADP, and adapt to most document-management and workpaper tools your firm uses. Because we operate in your existing stack and follow your file structure and referencing conventions, there's no migration and no new software for your team to learn.

How quickly can you turn around workpapers and PBC items during fieldwork?

Once onboarded, routine reconciliations and supporting schedules typically turn around within one to two business days, and same-day for follow-up requests during active fieldwork. We agree specific turnaround targets during scoping so your engagement calendar has firm, committed dates to plan around.

We're preparing for our first-ever audit. Can you help us get ready?

Yes. First audits are where preparation matters most. We build your reconciliations and lead schedules from the ground up, organize supporting documentation, walk through the PBC list line by line, and flag gaps before auditors do — so your first fieldwork experience is orderly rather than a scramble.

Will the same people work on our files each season, and how does pricing work?

Yes — you get a dedicated team that learns your templates and recurring judgment calls, so quality improves year over year. On pricing, we scope by engagement volume, complexity, and turnaround needs, then propose a dedicated-team or per-engagement model with transparent rates before any work begins.
