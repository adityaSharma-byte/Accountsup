Overview

Financial Statements for Canada

Your financial statements are the document every lender, investor, board member, and auditor reads first. AccountsUp produces complete, standard-compliant statement packages — balance sheet, income statement, cash flow, and equity — with footnote disclosures that withstand scrutiny. Whether you report under US GAAP or IFRS, our dedicated teams turn raw ledgers into board-ready reporting your stakeholders trust on sight.

Reporting rarely fails on the easy entries — it fails on the complex ones. Revenue recognition under ASC 606, lease accounting under ASC 842, business combinations, stock compensation, and consolidation are where errors hide and restatements begin. Our teams document the judgment behind every material position, so when an auditor or regulator asks how you arrived at a number, the answer is already written down.

We work inside the systems you already run — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, and Sage — pulling directly from your trial balance rather than asking you to re-export data into ours. AI-assisted reconciliation flags variances, missing accruals, and disclosure gaps before a human reviewer signs off, giving you the speed of automation with the accountability of a named CPA-led team behind every deliverable.

  • Audit and review engagements with fewer open items and review comments
  • Disclosures that stay current with the latest ASC and IFRS standards
  • Board- and lender-ready reporting your stakeholders trust on first read

Questions

Financial Statements, answered

Do you prepare statements under both US GAAP and IFRS?

Yes. We prepare full statement sets under US GAAP, IFRS, and IFRS for SMEs, and we handle GAAP-to-IFRS conversions with bridging schedules. This suits US entities with overseas parents or lenders, and international groups consolidating across multiple reporting frameworks.

How do you keep our financial data secure?

Your data stays in your systems wherever possible. We operate under enterprise-grade, SOC 2-aligned controls: role-based access, multi-factor authentication, encrypted transfer, and full audit trails on every action. Work happens in secured environments, and access is scoped strictly to the people on your engagement.

Which accounting platforms do you work with?

We work directly inside QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, and Sage, and pull payroll data from ADP where relevant. Rather than migrating you to our tools, we report from your trial balance in the system you already run, so your team keeps one source of truth.

Can you support our CPA firm's overflow during busy season?

Yes. Many clients are CPA firms that use us as an extension of their team for compilations, workpaper preparation, and disclosure drafting. We work under your review and branding, scaling capacity during peak season without you hiring, onboarding, and offboarding seasonal staff.

How do you handle complex transactions like revenue recognition or leases?

Each material transaction gets a documented position memo citing the relevant standard — ASC 606, ASC 842, business combinations, or stock compensation — and the judgment applied. When an auditor or regulator questions a number, the reasoning is already written down and ready to defend.

How long does onboarding take before the first statements?

Most engagements move from kickoff to first draft within two to three weeks, depending on how clean your trial balance is and how quickly system access is granted. Once templates and reconciliations are established, every subsequent reporting cycle runs meaningfully faster.
