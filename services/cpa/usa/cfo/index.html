Overview

Virtual CFO & Advisory for United States

Most growing companies hit a ceiling where bookkeeping tells you what happened but nobody translates it into what to do next. AccountsUp Virtual CFO service fills that gap with a dedicated finance lead and support team who own your forecasting, budgeting, and cash-flow planning — turning your QuickBooks, Xero, or NetSuite ledger into decisions you can defend in a board meeting.

You get more than reports. Your Virtual CFO builds a rolling 13-week cash-flow forecast, a driver-based annual budget, and live KPI dashboards that track the metrics that actually move your business — gross margin, burn, runway, unit economics, and working-capital cycles. Every number is reconciled to source, AI-checked for anomalies, and reviewed by a human expert before it reaches you.

When the numbers raise questions, your CFO is in the room to answer them. We prepare board packs, pressure-test fundraising and hiring plans, model pricing and scenario changes, and sit alongside your leadership as a fractional finance partner. You keep control of your stack and your data; we bring the discipline, foresight, and calm judgment of a seasoned CFO.

  • Cash-flow surprises replaced by a forecast you can see weeks ahead
  • A faster, cleaner monthly close with board packs ready on schedule
  • Clear unit economics and margin visibility that guide pricing and spend
  • Senior financial leadership in the room for every major decision

Questions

Virtual CFO & Advisory, answered

How is a Virtual CFO different from a bookkeeper or accountant?

A bookkeeper records transactions and an accountant closes the books; a Virtual CFO uses that data to look forward. We own forecasting, budgeting, cash-flow planning, and strategic advisory — translating your historicals into decisions about pricing, hiring, fundraising, and runway.

How much does a Virtual CFO engagement cost?

Engagements are scoped to your stage and complexity, typically as a fixed monthly retainer rather than hourly billing. Pricing depends on transaction volume, reporting cadence, and advisory depth. Most clients pay a fraction of a full-time CFO's salary — we share a transparent quote after a short discovery call.

Do I have to switch accounting software?

No. We work inside your existing stack — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, ADP, and the tools around them. There are no forced migrations. We connect through secure, read-appropriate access, keep your data where it lives, and build forecasting and dashboards on top of what you already run.

How do you keep our financial data secure?

Security is enterprise-grade and layered: encrypted data in transit and at rest, multi-factor authentication, role-based access limited to your engagement team, audit trails on every action, and staff operating under strict confidentiality. We align to SOC 2 and GDPR-style controls so your financials stay protected end to end.

How quickly can we get started and see value?

Onboarding usually takes two to four weeks to connect systems, clean the baseline, and stand up your first forecast, budget, and dashboards. Many clients see immediate value from the initial diagnostic, which surfaces cash risks and quick wins before the full engagement is even live.

Can you work as a white-label CFO behind our CPA firm?

Yes. Many CPA and accounting firms use us to extend advisory and Virtual CFO services to their own clients without hiring in-house. We operate discreetly behind your brand, follow your standards and deliverables, and let you offer higher-value advisory while we handle the modeling and reporting.
