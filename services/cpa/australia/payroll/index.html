Overview

Payroll Services for Australia & NZ

Payroll is where small errors become expensive, public problems. A misfiled deduction, a late direct deposit, or a wrong W-2 box erodes employee trust and invites penalty notices. AccountsUp runs your entire pay cycle end to end — gross-to-net calculation, direct deposit, statutory withholding, and every year-end filing — as a dedicated team working inside the payroll platform you already use.

Whether you are a CPA firm carrying dozens of client payrolls through each deadline or a growing company that has outgrown a spreadsheet, we plug into QuickBooks, Xero, ADP, Gusto, or Sage and take the recurring work off your desk. Every run is calculated once, checked by AI for outliers, and reviewed by an accountant before a single payment leaves your account.

Because payroll rules differ by country and by state or province, compliance is built into the workflow rather than bolted on afterward. We manage federal, state, and local tax deductions across the US, Canada, the UK, and Australia — including PAYE, CPP, EI, and superannuation — and keep a full audit trail of every calculation, remittance, and filing we touch.

  • Payroll that runs on time, every cycle, without weekend fire drills
  • Fewer costly errors and penalty notices, thanks to AI screening plus expert review
  • Reclaimed hours for your finance team to focus on advisory work instead of data entry
  • Audit-ready records and confident, on-deadline year-end filings across every jurisdiction

What's included

Everything the payroll services engagement covers

Full-Cycle Payroll Processing

Gross-to-net for salaried, hourly, and contract workers — overtime, bonuses, commissions, garnishments, and reimbursements calculated every cycle and reconciled before approval.

Direct Deposit & Payment Runs

Scheduled ACH, EFT, and Faster Payments runs so employees and contractors are paid on the exact date, with payslips generated and distributed automatically.

Statutory Deductions & Remittances

Federal, state, provincial, and local withholding — income tax, FICA, CPP, EI, PAYE, National Insurance, and super — deducted correctly and remitted to authorities on time.

Year-End Filings (W-2, 1099, T4)

Annual W-2, W-3, 1099-NEC, T4, T4A, P60, and Single Touch Payroll (STP) finalisation preparation, reconciliation to the general ledger, and filing with the IRS, CRA, HMRC, or ATO.

Compliance & Regulatory Monitoring

We track changing tax tables, minimum-wage rules, and filing deadlines across four countries, applying rate changes before they take effect so no run is ever out of date.

New Hire, Termination & Onboarding

W-4, TD1, and starter-declaration setup, benefits and deduction enrollment, final-pay calculations, and clean off-boarding so joiners and leavers are handled without a scramble.

Payroll Reconciliation & GL Posting

Each cycle is reconciled to bank activity and posted to the correct general-ledger accounts in your books, so payroll and accounting never drift apart.

Payroll Reporting & Analytics

Labor-cost, department, and headcount reports plus liability summaries delivered each cycle, giving finance leaders a clear view of where payroll spend is going.

How it works

A clear path from first call to steady delivery

01

Discovery & Setup

We map your pay schedules, employee types, deduction rules, and jurisdictions, then connect securely to your payroll platform and document a run checklist tailored to your business.

02

Parallel Run & Validation

Before going live, we process a trial cycle alongside your current method and reconcile the results line by line, so we catch discrepancies before real money moves.

03

Each Pay Cycle

You send changes — new hires, hours, bonuses, terminations — and we calculate, AI-check, and reconcile the run, then send it back for a single-click approval.

04

Payment, Remittance & Filing

On approval, we release direct deposits, remit statutory deductions to the authorities, and file periodic returns — then post everything to your general ledger.

05

Year-End & Ongoing Review

At year end we prepare and file W-2, 1099, T4, and equivalent forms, and we hold regular reviews to keep your setup accurate as rules change.

Questions

Payroll Services, answered

How much does outsourced payroll cost?

Pricing is transparent and scales with headcount and pay frequency, typically quoted as a base platform fee plus a per-employee, per-run rate. After a short discovery call we send a fixed monthly quote with no setup surprises, so you can budget with confidence from day one.

How do you keep our payroll data secure?

Your data stays protected by encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access limiting who can see what, mandatory multi-factor authentication, and detailed audit trails. Our teams work from secure, office-only environments under SOC 2 and ISO-aligned controls, and we sign confidentiality agreements before any data changes hands.

Which payroll platforms do you work with?

We operate inside the tools you already use — QuickBooks, Xero, ADP, Gusto, Sage, NetSuite, and most major payroll systems. There is no forced migration; we log in to your environment, so your historical data, reports, and integrations stay intact and you keep full ownership and visibility.

How long does onboarding take?

Most clients are live within two to four weeks. We spend the first week mapping your pay rules and jurisdictions, then run a parallel cycle against your current process to validate every calculation. Once the trial reconciles cleanly, we switch you to live processing with no gap in pay dates.

Can you handle payroll across multiple countries?

Yes. We process and file payroll in the US, Canada, the UK, and Australia, managing federal, state, and provincial deductions, PAYE and National Insurance, CPP and EI, and superannuation. Multi-region businesses get one partner and one consistent process instead of separate bureaus in each country.

What happens if there is a payroll error or a tax notice?

If a discrepancy surfaces, your dedicated team investigates it the same day, issues any correction run needed, and communicates directly with the tax authority on your behalf. Because every calculation is logged with a full audit trail, we can trace exactly what happened and resolve notices quickly.
