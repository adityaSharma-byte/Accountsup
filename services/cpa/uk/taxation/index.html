Overview

Tax Preparation for United Kingdom & Europe

Tax season shouldn't mean bottlenecks, late nights, or exposure. AccountsUp prepares individual and corporate returns end to end — from data intake and workpaper organization through preparation, review, and e-filing — for CPA firms and growing businesses across the US, Canada, UK, and Australia. Every return is built to be accurate, defensible, and filed on time, with liabilities minimized wherever the code legitimately allows.

Preparation is handled by a dedicated tax team that works directly inside your existing stack — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, and your preferred tax software — so nothing is re-keyed and every figure ties back to source. AI-assisted checks flag missing forms, mismatched carryforwards, and reconciliation gaps before a human reviewer signs off, combining machine consistency with the professional judgment complex returns require.

Beyond filing, we treat tax as a year-round discipline. Quarterly estimates, provisions, entity-structure questions, and cross-border obligations are planned ahead of deadlines rather than reconciled after them. When a jurisdiction changes a rule or a client crosses a new nexus threshold, your team already knows — so compliance stays current across every state, province, and country you operate in, and surprises at year-end become rare.

  • A faster, calmer filing season with returns delivered ahead of deadlines
  • Fewer notices, amendments, and rework from accurate, reviewed returns
  • Lower tax liability captured through year-round, proactive planning
  • Partner and senior-staff capacity freed from preparation for higher-value advisory

What's included

Everything the tax preparation engagement covers

Individual & Personal Returns

Federal, state, and provincial personal returns — 1040, T1, SA100, and equivalents — including multi-state, self-employment, investment, and rental income schedules.

Corporate & Pass-Through Returns

C-corp, S-corp, partnership, and LLC filings — 1120, 1120-S, 1065, T2, and CT600 — with book-to-tax reconciliation, K-1s, and supporting schedules.

Tax Planning & Advisory

Year-round scenario modeling on entity choice, timing, deductions, and distributions — projecting liability early so clients can act before the year closes, not after.

Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance

Federal, state, provincial, and cross-border obligations tracked in one place — including nexus monitoring, apportionment, and treaty positions across the US, Canada, UK, and Australia.

Estimated Payments & Provisions

Quarterly estimate calculations, safe-harbor tracking, and current/deferred tax provisions — so cash-flow planning and financial statements reflect the real liability throughout the year.

Year-End Filing & Close Support

Coordinated year-end workpapers, adjusting entries, and e-filing that keep every deadline — from extensions to final submission — organized, tracked, and delivered on schedule.

Notices & Authority Correspondence

Response drafting and documentation for IRS, CRA, HMRC, and ATO notices — reconciling the disputed items and preparing defensible support so issues resolve quickly.

Prior-Year Review & Amendments

Diagnostic review of prior filings to catch missed deductions, credits, or errors — with amended returns prepared and documented where a refund or correction is warranted.

Questions

Tax Preparation, answered

How much does tax preparation outsourcing cost?

Most engagements price per return by complexity, or as a monthly retainer for firms with steady volume. A straightforward personal return costs far less than a multi-state corporate filing. After a short scoping call, you receive a transparent, itemized quote with no per-form surprises.

How do you keep our clients' tax data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, protected by multi-factor authentication, role-based access, and full audit trails. Our teams work only from controlled, office-based environments — never personal devices — and access is scoped to the specific engagement, then revoked when it ends.

Which tax software and accounting platforms do you work in?

We work directly inside your existing stack — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, and Sage for accounting, plus the professional tax software your firm already files with. There's no migration and no new tool to learn; we adapt to your process rather than forcing ours onto you.

How quickly can you onboard us before filing season?

Most clients are live within one to two weeks. Onboarding covers entity mapping, secure portal setup, access to your systems, and a review of prior-year returns. Starting a few weeks ahead of a deadline gives us room to organize documents and plan the workload comfortably.

Do you handle returns across multiple countries and states?

Yes. We prepare and file across the US, Canada, UK, and Australia — including multi-state and multi-provincial returns, nexus and apportionment, and cross-border positions. One team tracks every jurisdiction your clients or entities touch, so obligations don't slip through the gaps between them.

Are you replacing our staff or supporting them?

Supporting them. We act as an extension of your team, handling preparation and first-pass review so your partners and senior staff focus on advisory, client relationships, and final sign-off. You keep control of every position and approval; we absorb the volume and the deadline pressure.
